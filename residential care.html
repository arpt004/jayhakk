Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Residential Care services

Who can access Jayhawkk’s Residential Care Support?

Our services are available to older adults, individuals recovering from illness or surgery, and anyone needing extra help due to disability or health conditions.

How flexible are the care schedules at Jayhawkk?

We offer flexible care visits tailored to your needs, from brief check-ins to multiple visits per day, including morning and evening routines.

Does Jayhawkk help with meal preparation?

Yes, our carers prepare meals and snacks, ensuring they meet dietary preferences and nutritional needs.

How does Jayhawkk support medication management?

We provide medication reminders and help monitor adherence to prescribed treatment plans to ensure safety and consistency.

Can Jayhawkk carers assist with mobility?

Absolutely. Our team supports safe movement within the home, including walking, transfers, and use of mobility aids.

What household tasks are included in Jayhawkk’s care?

Our carers help with light cleaning, laundry, and maintaining a safe, tidy home environment.

Is companionship part of Jayhawkk’s care approach?

We offer flexible care visits tailored to your needs, from brief check-ins to multiple visits per day, including morning and evening routines.

How does Jayhawkk ensure quality and safety?

All carers undergo thorough training, background checks, and ongoing supervision. Jayhawkk creates personalised care plans and regularly reviews them with clients and families.

How can I start receiving Residential Care Support from Jayhawkk?

Simply contact Jayhawkk Domiciliary Care Agency to arrange a free assessment. Our team will work with you to design a care plan tailored to your specific needs.
